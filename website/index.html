Divvy

Scan a receipt, assign items to friends, split the bill. Powered by AI.

Status: In App Store Review

Divvy is under review by Apple and will be available on the App Store soon. The app includes a 4-tier ad system, AI-powered receipt scanning, and shareable web links for splitting bills with friends.

How It Works

  • Scan - Take a photo of any receipt and AI extracts every item and price instantly
  • Split - Assign items to friends so everyone pays for exactly what they ordered
  • Share - Send a link so friends can see their share and pay you through Venmo, PayPal, or Cash App

Free to Use

Divvy is completely free. Watch a short ad to earn a scan credit — no subscriptions, no hidden fees, no paywalls. Your first scan is on us. If ads aren't available, you'll get a free daily pass so you're never stuck.

About

Divvy was built to solve a simple problem: splitting a restaurant bill shouldn't require a calculator and a group chat argument. Point your camera at the receipt and let AI handle the rest.
